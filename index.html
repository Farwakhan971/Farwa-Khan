Remote Cashier

Built the Remote Cashier App to enable restaurant agents to work remotely by handling customer orders through video calls. Customers use in-restaurant terminals to connect with agents, and terminal statuses (active, busy, inactive) are updated in real time for the agents. Agents can initiate a call by clicking on an active terminal. For security, a PIN screen appears on the terminal whenever a customer moves the app to the background or during terminal unsetting, ensuring customers cannot access other apps on the device or unset the terminal Managers can monitor calls, join video call if needed, and manage agents, terminals, and restaurants through a dashboard. The system supports agent breaks, login and logout, and automatically sends email alerts when all agents from a restaurant are on break. Real-time dashboard charts display active agents and terminals by restaurant. The app also tracks agent check-in and check-out activity. See more...
